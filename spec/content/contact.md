Browse our five tour options — ghost and haunted history, historic walking, food and spirits, celebrity homes and filming locations, and private custom. Check pricing, duration, and what each tour covers, then pick the one (or two) that fits your visit.

## Meeting Point

### H2: Where We Meet

All tours depart from downtown Franklin, Tennessee. The exact meeting point — within easy walking distance of Main Street — is included in your booking confirmation. Downtown Franklin has free parking in both the public garages and street spaces throughout the historic district. It's one of the most accessible small-town downtowns you'll visit, and parking is never a headache.

If you're coming from Nashville, Franklin is about 30 minutes south on I-65. Most Nashville hotel concierges know the route well.

## Booking FAQ

### H2: Before You Book

**H3: How far in advance should I book?**
For weekend tours and any tour in October, we recommend booking at least 72 hours ahead — they fill quickly. For private tours, a week's notice is ideal so Diana can build your itinerary. Weekday tours during off-peak months can sometimes be booked with 24 hours' notice.

**H3: What is your cancellation policy?**
Cancellations made 48 hours or more before the tour start time receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable, but we'll do our best to reschedule if possible. If we cancel due to weather or circumstances on our end, you'll receive a full refund.

**H3: Can I pay on the day?**
We prefer advance payment to hold your spot, but if you're in town and want to join a tour that has availability, call us and we'll sort it out.

**H3: Do you accommodate dietary restrictions on the food tour?**
Yes — let us know when you book and Diana will make sure each stop on the food tour can accommodate your needs. Vegetarian options are available at every stop.

**H3: Can I book as a gift?**
Absolutely. Gift certificates are available for any tour. Contact us directly to arrange.
