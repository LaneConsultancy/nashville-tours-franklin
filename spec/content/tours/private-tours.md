## Who Books Private Tours

### H2: Perfect For Every Occasion

**Bachelorette parties and girls' trips.** Franklin has become one of the South's most popular bachelorette destinations, and private tours are one of the best ways to make a weekend here genuinely memorable rather than interchangeable with every other Southern bachelorette town. Diana can build an evening ghost tour, a celebrity homes and food combination, or a full-afternoon history immersion — whatever fits the group and the tone of the weekend.

**Couples and anniversary celebrations.** An anniversary, a honeymoon, a milestone birthday — occasions that deserve more than a generic group experience. A private tour can be shaped around what matters to you specifically, including the spots in Franklin that have personal significance.

**Family reunions.** Multi-generational groups are one of Diana's favorite private tour clients. She's experienced with groups that range from grandparents to grandchildren and knows how to find the level of detail and engagement that works across a wide age range. Franklin's Civil War history, architecture, and stories land differently for different generations — a private tour accommodates all of them.

**Corporate team outings.** A private Franklin tour is a genuinely different corporate experience — one that gives a team shared context about a remarkable place rather than a ropes course or a bowling alley. History tours, food experiences, and combined programs work well for corporate groups who want something that generates real conversation.

**Out-of-town guests.** When you have visitors who deserve to see Franklin properly — not the tourist version, but the real version — a private tour is the most efficient way to make that happen. Diana covers in two hours what would take most people days of wandering to discover.

**Photographers and content creators.** Franklin's historic district, architecture, and residential areas are visually exceptional. A private tour can be paced around photographic opportunities, with Diana positioning the group at the right spots at the right times.

## How Customisation Works

### H2: Building Your Tour

The process is a conversation, not a form.

**Step 1: Reach out.** Contact Diana via phone or email with the basics: occasion, approximate group size, date, and what interests you. A five-minute conversation is usually enough to establish the shape of the tour.

**Step 2: Diana designs your itinerary.** Based on your interests and the time available, Diana puts together a proposed tour route and focus. She'll suggest the combination of stops, themes, and experiences that makes most sense for your group.

**Step 3: Confirm and prepare.** Once you've agreed on the itinerary, Diana confirms the booking, sends practical information about meeting points and what to bring, and handles any special arrangements.

**Step 4: Your tour.** No other guests, no competing agendas, and no rushing. Diana guides your group at your pace through the Franklin she knows best.

## FAQ

### H2: Private Tour Questions Answered

**H3: How far in advance do I need to book?**
A week's advance notice is ideal for most private tours — it gives Diana time to build the itinerary and make any necessary arrangements. For large groups or complex multi-component tours, two weeks or more is preferred. For simpler tours with shorter notice, get in touch and we'll see what's possible.

**H3: What is the minimum group size?**
There is no minimum. Couples book private tours regularly. The minimum pricing is per group, not per person, so a tour for two works out more economically than it might appear.

**H3: Can I request specific themes or topics?**
Yes — that's the point of a private tour. If you have a particular interest in the Carter House, in the celebrity homes, in Franklin's food scene, in the architectural history, in the haunted sites, or in any combination thereof, Diana will build around it.

**H3: What is the cancellation policy for private tours?**
Private tours cancelled 72 hours or more before the tour receive a full refund. Cancellations within 72 hours are non-refundable, but we will do our best to reschedule if possible.

**H3: Can you accommodate accessibility requirements?**
Yes. Let Diana know about any accessibility needs when booking — she'll design the tour route and format to accommodate them.

**H3: Is there a minimum booking duration?**
Private tours are available from two hours. For bespoke experiences that combine multiple tour types, plan for three to four hours.
