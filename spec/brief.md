## Business Description
Nashville Tours Franklin is a premium tour company based in historic Franklin, Tennessee, offering curated walking tours, ghost tours, food experiences, and private guided adventures. Founded by Diana Berry, the company bridges Nashville's visitor pipeline to Franklin's rich history, Southern charm, and celebrity culture. Unlike commodity walking tour operators, Nashville Tours Franklin positions as the story-driven, local-expert experience for visitors who want more than a standard group walk.

- **Slug:** historic-walking-tour

- **Departure:** Downtown Franklin

## Design Aesthetic
**Premium Agency** (soft-skill variant)

This is an experiential tourism brand. The site needs to feel:
- Warm, inviting, and Southern — but polished and premium
- Story-driven with atmospheric photography
- Trust-building but not corporate
- Easy to book, clear pricing, no friction

**Sub-vibe:** Editorial Luxury — serif headlines, warm color palette, generous whitespace, photographic storytelling
